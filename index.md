 
 
 
## Project #1
### Half-adder

The breadboard contains a half adder using an XOR gate and an AND gates.

Inputs are controlled with pushbuttons.

The outputs are connected to LEDs to easily
observe their states.

- Power: 5V
- Red LED: 330Ω
- Red LED: 220Ω (probably)
